Biography

Full Name:Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro

Date of Birth:February 5,1985

Place of Birth:Funchal,Madeira,Portugal

Early Life: Ronaldo grew up in a humble background and showed an early passion in football . his talent was noticed at a young age ,and by the age of 12 , he moved to Lisbon to join Sporting CP's youth academy.

Career Highlights:Ronaldo's career began at Sporting CP,but he rose to international prominence after joining Manchester United in 2003.He went to Real madrid,Where he became the club's all-time top scorer,before joining Juventus in 2018.In 2021,he returned to Manchester United, and later moved to Al Nassar in 2023.
